GUEST LECTURE POSTER — INFORMATION

Title: Talk to Your Documents: Building a Real-World AI Assistant
Speaker: Dr Mohammad Arifur Rahman, Analyst Programmer, Wimmera CMA, Victoria
Course: ICT108
Date: Tuesday 14 July 2026
Time: 2:00 pm – 3:00 pm
Format: In person and live-streamed (online)

About the session:
A case study of a real AI product, a document question-answering chatbot,
built step by step, with each concept introduced through a real problem
encountered while building it. Covers embeddings, retrieval (RAG),
hallucination, and reliability.

Who should attend:
- Requested: ICT108 students
- Welcomed: all IT students and staff
- Open to industry stakeholders via live stream

Why attend:
- See how a real-world AI system is designed and built
- Understand core ideas: embeddings, retrieval (RAG), hallucination, reliability
- Follow the journey from a naive bot to a trustworthy assistant
